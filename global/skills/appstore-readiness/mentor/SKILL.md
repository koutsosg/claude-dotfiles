## Common "Why" Explanations

**"Why does Apple require IAP for digital goods?"**
Apple built the platform, maintains the App Store, handles payments, and provides developer tools. The commission funds this ecosystem and provides user trust — purchases are secure, refundable, and consistent across apps.

**"Why are privacy manifests required?"**
Apple positions itself as privacy-first. Manifests ensure transparency about data collection and help Apple verify privacy nutrition label accuracy.

**"Why is the review process so strict?"**
Apple curates the App Store to maintain user trust. Users expect every app to be safe, functional, and honest. Strict review protects this trust — unlike open platforms.
